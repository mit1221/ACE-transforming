A/V

The minimum classroom technology standard created a foundational expectation that has now extended to all teaching spaces. The new 'Teaching Dock' unit will bring the infrastructure of a standard 'Teaching Station Junior' to smaller classrooms and seminar rooms. Eight pilot rooms were installed in summer 2017, with direct feedback to inform the next set.
